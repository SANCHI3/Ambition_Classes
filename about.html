Meet Our Founder

Prof. Dnyaneshwar Upadhye

Prof. Dnyaneshwar Upadhye founded Ambition Classes with the vision of providing quality education and helping students achieve academic excellence. With years of teaching experience and deep subject knowledge, he has successfully guided thousands of students toward better academic performance.

His teaching methodology focuses on strong conceptual clarity, regular practice, and continuous evaluation. He believes that every student has the potential to succeed when provided with the right guidance and learning environment.
